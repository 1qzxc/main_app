One of my friends works as an SMM manager for a company that creates Neon Signs. Me and my girlfriend, we have developed and impemented new fox face and colouring with the page design.

She wanted to have custom built landing page that would have the carousel on the page to show the example neon signs with approximate prices. I have used JQuery to render that.

She also wanted the page to have a form that when filed out sends a letter to her email with the data from the form. For this task I have used PHP script on the page that uses Postfix to send the email.
